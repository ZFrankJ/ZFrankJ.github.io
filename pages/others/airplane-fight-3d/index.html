1P: take off, intercept the naval raider, and stop it before it strikes or escapes.
2P: split-screen duel; P1 defends the base while P2 attacks with one missile, then escapes. Scale: 1u = 10 m; aircraft speed range 3-18u/s.
Background P1 is a land-based interceptor scrambling from the runway. The navy raider carries one heavy missile to break the runways, then tries to escape the 35 km combat radius.
1 Player Arrow keys turn and pitch. Q / E adjust throttle. Space fires twin wing guns. C switches cockpit / chase view. X continues the chase after base loss.
2 Players P1 uses WASD + Space. P2 uses Arrow keys + Enter. Right Shift launches P2's missile toward the gunsight aim point.
System 1 / 2 choose mode. 7 / 8 / 9 choose weather. F toggles fullscreen. R or Space restarts after game over.
